Week 1 of 4
Week 1

Strategy & Setup

Alignment and foundations. This is where we set expectations and design the system that will capture guest data inside your restaurant.

What you'll do
  • Attend Call #1 (Overview & QR Code Planning) with your dedicated Account Manager
  • Review goals, success metrics, and overall strategy
  • Finalize QR code placement and usage inside your restaurant
  • Set up billing (ACH + backup card)
Who you'll talk to

Your Dedicated Account Manager -- your main point of contact throughout the entire process.

What we're doing internally
  • Building your Dishio account (menus, smart site, flows)
  • Preparing QR codes tied directly to your Dishio system
  • Initiating QR code printing or shipment
This step matters because everything else builds on the Dishio system.

02
Week 2 of 4
Week 2

Technical Integrations

Making sure all systems "talk" to each other. We need to integrate to your key systems such as POS, online orders, reservation system, etc.

What you'll do
  • Attend Call #2 (Technical Setup & Integrations)
Who you'll talk to
  • Integration Specialist (technical setup)
  • Account Manager stays involved and project manages
What we're doing internally
  • Integrating Dishio with POS system, online ordering, reservation platforms
  • Validating data flow and tracking
  • Finalizing backend setup
By the end of this week, Dishio is fully connected to your existing tech stack.

Week 3 of 4
Week 3

Activation & Ad Strategy

Turning the system on and adding fuel. Your QR codes go live in-store and we finalize your ad campaigns.

What you'll do
  • Attend Call #3 (QR Code Implementation + Ad Strategy)
  • Review campaign direction and targeting
Who you'll talk to
  • Your Account Manager
  • Media strategy input supported by our Media Buying Team
What we're doing internally
  • QR codes arrive and are activated in-store
  • Staff training on QR usage (if needed)
  • Building ad creative and campaign assets
  • Finalizing ad strategy tied directly to Dishio data
At this point, the system is live in-store -- ads are what scale it.

Week 4

Final Review & Go-Live

Approval and launch. Everything comes together -- your system is live, your ads are ready, and your growth engine is about to turn on.

What you'll do
  • Review ads and strategy
  • Give us the thumbs up to go live
  • No call required unless changes are needed
Who's involved
  • Your Account Manager
  • Media Buying Team
What happens internally
  • Ads launch on Meta & Google
  • Monthly billing officially begins
  • Ongoing optimization and reporting starts
Your account is now fully onboarded and actively driving traffic, collecting data, and generating revenue.
