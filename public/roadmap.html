Phase 01

Month 1: Learning Phase & QR Optimization

What's Happening
  • Ad campaigns launch across Meta, Google, and programmatic
  • QR codes are placed and tracked for in-store attribution
  • Baseline performance data is being collected
  • A/B testing begins on audiences, creatives, and messaging
What You'll Do
  • Approve initial ad creatives and messaging
  • Confirm QR code placements in-store
  • Validate that POS tracking is connected and accurate
What We're Doing
  • Setting up and monitoring all ad campaigns daily
  • Configuring QR tracking and conversion attribution
  • Building your first performance dashboards
  • Testing multiple audience segments to find winners
Everything from Month 2 forward is built on the data we collect now.

How We Prove ROI

Every ad dollar is tracked from impression to revenue in your POS

Ad Impression
→
Click
→
Conversion Action
→
Revenue in Your POS
Reservations
Conversion: Booking completed
Revenue Tie: Avg. check per cover x party size
In Reports: Cost per reservation, revenue per ad dollar
Online Orders
Conversion: Order placed
Revenue Tie: Direct order value from POS
In Reports: ROAS, avg. order value, repeat rate
Store Visits
Conversion: QR scan or foot traffic
Revenue Tie: POS transactions within visit window
In Reports: Cost per visit, attributed revenue
Catering & Events
Conversion: Form submission or call
Revenue Tie: Catering invoice value
In Reports: Lead cost, close rate, revenue per lead

Phase 02

Months 2-3: Scaling & Budget Optimization

What's Happening
  • Month 1 data informs budget reallocation and targeting refinements
  • Winning audiences and creatives are scaled up
  • Underperforming campaigns are paused or restructured
  • New ad formats and placements are tested
What You'll Do
  • Review biweekly performance reports
  • Provide feedback on updated ad creatives
  • Share any upcoming promotions or seasonal events
  • Approve budget adjustments based on performance data
What We're Doing
  • Optimizing bids and budgets across all channels
  • Scaling proven audience segments and lookalikes
  • Running monthly creative refreshes
  • Building detailed attribution models for revenue tracking
By end of Month 3, we'll have a strong, data-backed picture of what's driving revenue.
